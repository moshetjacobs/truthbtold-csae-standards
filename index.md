# TruthBTold.Space – Standards Against Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE)

**Last updated: January 2026**

TruthBTold.Space, operated by beatacoin.com, is committed to maintaining a safe environment for all users. We enforce a strict zero‑tolerance policy toward child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). Our platform must never be used to harm, endanger, or exploit minors in any form.

---

## 1. Zero‑Tolerance Policy

TruthBTold.Space prohibits:

- Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
- Sexualized content involving minors
- Grooming, enticement, or solicitation of minors
- Attempts to contact minors for sexual purposes
- Any behavior that facilitates, promotes, or attempts CSAE

Any violation results in immediate account removal and reporting to the appropriate authorities.

---

## 2. Reporting and Enforcement

Users may report harmful content or behavior through:

- In‑app reporting tools  
- Email: info@beatacoin.com

All reports are reviewed promptly. Confirmed CSAM is reported to the **National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC)** or the appropriate authority in the relevant jurisdiction, in accordance with applicable laws.

---

## 3. Cooperation With Law Enforcement

TruthBTold.Space fully cooperates with law enforcement agencies investigating CSAE. We comply with all legal obligations related to child protection, data preservation, and reporting.

---

## 4. Safety Measures

To protect users and prevent CSAE, TruthBTold.Space maintains:

- Moderation processes to detect and remove harmful content  
- Systems to block repeat or high‑risk offenders  
- Age‑appropriate restrictions and safety controls  
- Staff trained to identify, escalate, and respond to CSAE concerns  

We continuously evaluate and improve our safety practices.

---

For urgent concerns related to child safety or CSAE, contact:
